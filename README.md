
# ⚖️ AIN – An Intelligent Legal Assistant for Bangladesh

> **AIN (আইন)** is a zero-cost AI-powered legal assistant designed to make Bangladeshi laws more accessible through Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

## 📖 Overview

AIN is an AI-powered legal assistant that aims to simplify legal information for the people of Bangladesh by providing intelligent, context-aware responses based on authentic legal documents.

The system utilizes **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** to retrieve relevant legal knowledge before generating responses, improving both accuracy and reliability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- 📚 Make Bangladeshi laws easy to understand
- 🤖 Provide AI-powered legal assistance
- 🔍 Retrieve information from authentic legal documents

- 🌐 Support multilingual legal queries
